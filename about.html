About Us



Since 2002 we are authorized dealers and suppliers of leading and reputed brands of Decorative Glass, all types of Glass and Mirrors, Silicone and Adhesive, Glass Hardware, Patch Fittings etc. We can customize supplies of Glass as per your requirement.

We have well experienced Installation team to undertake installation of Toughened Glasses, Mirrors, Lacquered Glasses etc. We can also provide customization as per your requirements.

With our vast experience in Glass field of over 18 years we can assist in providing solutions for all your Glass requirements.
